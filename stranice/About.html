Origins

The Twilight Traverse Fair began as a humble gathering in the quaint town of Glenwood, nestled between rolling
hills and lush forests. Originating over a century ago, its inception was rooted in a desire to celebrate the
magical transition between day and night, when the sky is painted in hues of purple and gold.


Initially a small community affair, the fair quickly gained traction, drawing visitors from neighboring towns
and beyond with its unique blend of enchantment and excitement. Over the years, it evolved into a grand
spectacle, boasting an array of attractions to delight all who attended.



What we offer

From exhilarating carnival rides to captivating performances by local artists, the Twilight Traverse Fair became
synonymous with joy and wonder. Its signature event, the Twilight Parade, featured intricately decorated floats
illuminated by twinkling lights, weaving through the streets as music filled the air.



Twilight Traverse today

As time passed, the fair continued to thrive, adapting to changing tastes and trends while remaining true to its
roots. Today, the Twilight Traverse Fair stands as a cherished tradition, beloved by generations who eagerly
anticipate its arrival each year, ready to embark on a journey of delight and discovery as the sun sets and the
magic unfolds once more.



What to expect in the future?

In the years to come, the Twilight Traverse Fair envisions an even brighter future, embracing innovation while
preserving its timeless charm. With eco-friendly initiatives, immersive technologies, and a commitment to
inclusivity, it aims to enchant future generations, ensuring that the magic of dusk continues to unite
communities in joy and wonder for many years ahead.
